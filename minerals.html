Minerals and Commodities

Gold Dore Bars & Rough Diamond

We have established credible relationships with some local miners in Africa including Botswana, Guinea, Ghana, South Africa, Angola, and Sierra Leone, and we source and supply high quality Gold Dore Bars and Rough Diamond to our customer.

We market Gold and Diamond with responsibility, and only deal in conflict-free diamonds in accordance with the Kimberly Process Certification Scheme (KPCS).

Cobalt

In Consortium with local companies in the Democratic Republic of Congo, and our Partners, we are processing concession and mining rights for cabalt, copper, gold and diamond in the Democratic Republic of Congo to increase our supply base to our international customers.

Turkish-made products

In collaboration with our Partners, GoldenTime LLC, Turkey, our company is involved in export marketing and sales of a wide range of Turkish made industrial products including machinery, chemical products, electrical and electronic products, building materials, hospital supplies, medical materials, household supplies, food products, agricultural products, textiles and clothings, and many other high quality Turkish products for export.
