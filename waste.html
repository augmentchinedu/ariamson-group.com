Waste Management Services

Waste Management Services

Ariamson offers fully integrated waste management services and solutions designed to maximise resource recovery and minimise waste disposal to landfill. We undertake waste collection, treatment, recycling and technologically-driven energy generation services. Through the expertise of our Consortium, we promote and deliver sustainable waste management solutions, and ultimately turn waste to wealth and energy. Waste is no longer rubbish, but a resource that can be recycled or used to create sustainable energy, adding value to businesses and revenues to Governments. Prioritising recycling and energy generation, we have developed a six-stage process for the management of every kind of waste.

Recovery, Energy and Products Conversion

Our vision is to maximize the potential of waste to generate energy, and conversion into various products and raw materials including fertilizer, methane gas and electricity. For example, our lead technical partner’s most efficient and environmentally friendly incinerator plant produces electricity and steam.

Landfill - High Technological and Environmental friendly System

Against the current practice where wastes are heaped at “refuse dumps”, burnt or traditionally landfill, we would put in place a landfill facility that is technologically high and environment friendly. We would do more on waste treatment to meet the increased need to re-use and recycle waste so that there is less reliance on landfill. High technological and environmental friendly landfills sites would be opened involving local communities both during and after the sites working life in order to enhance the local environment by delivering high standards of restoration, robust aftercare systems, encourage biodiversity and where possible, facilitate public access and recreation, in addition to jobs creation.

Waste Treatment

Our facilities here include mechanical and biological treatment systems. Mechanical treatment is used to separate waste materials for recycling from mixed wastes, and to generate solid recovered fuel from which energy can be recovered. Biological treatment includes anaerobic digestion plants that treat food, catering, health care and other organic waste and yield renewable energy, and compost that can be returned to agriculture.

Non-recoverable Waste Management

Recycling and treatment processes contain some materials that cannot be recycled or used to recover energy, or arise as by-products of these processes. We manage the disposal of these waste materials sustainably, recover as much energy in the form of landfill gas combustion as is practical while minimising their environmental impact.

Collection and Logistics

We are continually developing our logistical capabilities to manage the waste collection and removal of individual and mixed materials and making them ready for recycling or energy generation.
