Infrastructure

Our infrastructural services relate to social and economic infrastructure and also energy related projects.

Energy & Power

In Ariamson, we have the capacity to analyse power generation, transmission and distribution infrastructures and offer appropriate advice on establishing plants and solutions that will resolve any present and future power issues.

We are also able to carry out various studies depending on need including: study of current loads and generation capacity in a country or region, network capacity, reliability and security, options for increasing capacity of existing lines, connecting distributed generation – long lines, control, protection, fault levels, and stability. In addition, we undertake feasibility studies in respect of power provision (both on grid and off grid) and provide bankable feasibility studies for funding purposes.

We are currently in talks with some of our European and Canadian Partners on alternative energy sources which are not only affordable, sustainable but environmentally friendly.

Services:

  • Consulting
  • Power generation and distribution

In collaboration with our Partners, we supply and market indoor and outdoor electrical products, including vacuum circuit breakers, aluminium conductors, switchgear panels, and power transformers.

Real Estate and Mass Housing

Ariamson’s properties vision is to be in the forefront of real estate development in Nigeria, providing high quality, most comfortable and affordable residential and commercial properties across the country. In particular, we are involved in the building of high quality and affordable housing for high, medium and low income earners.

We are focused on property development and management, marketing and sales of real estate products, and facility management. In partnership with some global brands in real estate in the UK, USA, UAE, and Asia, we are disrupting the real estate landscape in some urban cities in Nigeria, and delivering value for money to our customers.

Hotel Chains

We are concluding arrangements to establish 5 Star Hotels Chain in collaboration with our European Partners of 20 years world-wide outstanding experience in the hospital industry. The project which is phased into three main stages is expected to begin in the third quarter of 2020. We would be offering the highest international standards and luxuries, with some of the finest architecture, ambience, accommodation, and amenities. These hotels would be in Nigeria and South Africa.
